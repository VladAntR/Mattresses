Guarantee
  • The warranty period of furniture for HoReCa is 18 months. The service life begins from the moment of receipt of products by the customer.Warranty repair and maintenance of furniture is carried out within the current legislation of Ukraine.
  • Warranty service provides free repair of furniture in the event that the detected defect is caused by improper quality of materials used or factory defects. Warranty repair is carried out by replacing or eliminating defective elements (products).
  • The terms of repair are from 3 months, depending on the nature of the damage. But we always go to meet the client and try to eliminate the problem as soon as possible.
  • In the absence of the required material in the warehouse of the Manufacturer, the repair of the product is postponed for the time required for ordering and delivery of this part.
